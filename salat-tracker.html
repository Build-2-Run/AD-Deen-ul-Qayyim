⚠️ Makruh (Karahat) Times

📖 Sun Angle Notes

🌅 Ishraq Time Begins ~20 minutes after sunrise (Tulu'). Praying nafl at this time is highly recommended — 2 rak'ahs equals the reward of Hajj and Umrah.
☀️ Zawal / Istiwa When the sun reaches its zenith (arc 90°). Prayer is makruh during this brief period — typically 5–10 minutes before Dhuhr Adhan.
📐 Asr Indicator Asr begins when shadow of an object = its own height + shadow at Zawal. This corresponds to sun at ~35° elevation (approx arc 130°) in summer.
🌇 Makruh Before Maghrib When the sun turns yellow/reddish (typically 20–30 min before Maghrib). Nafl prayers become makruh; obligatory prayers remain valid.
