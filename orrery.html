🌙 Moon & Islamic Calendar

Allah says in the Quran: يَسْأَلُونَكَ عَنِ الْأَهِلَّةِ ۖ قُلْ هِيَ مَوَاقِيتُ لِلنَّاسِ وَالْحَجِّ

"They ask you about the crescents. Say: They are measurements of time for the people and for Hajj." — Al-Baqarah 2:189

  • 🌑 New Moon: Start of new Hijri month (after Hilal sighting)
  • 🌕 Full Moon: Mid-month (15 Ramadan, 15 Sha'ban)
  • 🌙 Lunar cycle: 29.5 days → 12 months = 354-day year
