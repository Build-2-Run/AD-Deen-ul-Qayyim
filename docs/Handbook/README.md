# AD-Deen ul-Qayyim Handbook

> *The Design, Product, Knowledge & Engineering Handbook*

---

## About this Handbook

The AD-Deen ul-Qayyim Handbook is the central source of truth for the project.

It documents not only how AD-Deen ul-Qayyim is built, but why it exists.

Every major decision—whether related to design, engineering, educational methodology, or product strategy—must first be reflected in this handbook before it becomes part of the application.

The handbook is intended to evolve alongside the project.

---

## Purpose

This handbook exists to ensure that AD-Deen ul-Qayyim remains:

- Authentic
- Consistent
- Beautiful
- Maintainable
- Educational
- Purpose-driven

Technology changes.

Design trends change.

Frameworks change.

The mission does not.
