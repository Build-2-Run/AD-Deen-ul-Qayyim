# Declaration

## In the Name of Allah, the Most Merciful, the Most Compassionate

Every meaningful project begins with an intention.

AD-Deen ul-Qayyim began with one sincere intention:

> To understand Islam more deeply.

This project was not started to compete with other websites.

It was not started to build a business.

It was not started to impress people.

It began because its founder wanted a place where authentic Islamic knowledge could be understood through careful study, meaningful reflection, and thoughtful connections with the world around us.

The Qur'an repeatedly invites mankind to observe the signs of Allah in creation, history, and within themselves.

AD-Deen ul-Qayyim exists to help connect those signs with authentic Islamic knowledge.

Knowledge should not remain isolated.

Prayer should connect with astronomy.

History should connect with revelation.

Nature should inspire reflection.

Science should encourage appreciation of Allah's creation while remaining grounded in authentic Islamic teachings.

Every feature should increase understanding.

Every visualization should clarify.

Every calculation should educate.

Every design decision should support learning.

If this project benefits even one person in understanding Islam correctly, then all praise belongs to Allah alone.

If mistakes are found, they should be corrected with humility and sincerity.

---

> "My Lord, increase me in knowledge."

(Qur'an 20:114)
