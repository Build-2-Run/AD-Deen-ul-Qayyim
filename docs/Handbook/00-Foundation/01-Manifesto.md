# AD-Deen ul-Qayyim Manifesto

AD-Deen ul-Qayyim is built upon one belief:

Technology should make beneficial knowledge easier to understand, not more difficult.

We believe that knowledge deserves excellence.

We believe that beautiful design can encourage learning without becoming a distraction.

We believe that authenticity must always come before aesthetics.

We believe that understanding is more valuable than information alone.

We believe that the signs of Allah are found throughout creation and deserve thoughtful reflection.

We believe that differences of scholarly opinion should be presented respectfully.

We believe that every feature should exist for a reason.

We believe that complexity belongs behind the interface, not in front of the user.

We believe that learning should inspire worship.

We believe that every improvement should move the project closer to its mission.

---

## Our Commitments

We commit to authenticity before popularity.

We commit to evidence before assumptions.

We commit to excellence before convenience.

We commit to continuous improvement.

We commit to humility.

Knowledge belongs to Allah.

Any benefit comes from Him.

Any mistake is our own.
