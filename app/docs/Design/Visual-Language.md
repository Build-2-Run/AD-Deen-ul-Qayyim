# Visual Language

This document defines how every screen within the ADQ platform should *feel*. While the platform spans diverse modules (Readers, Calculators, Maps), every page must feel like a unified member of the ADQ family, bound by the philosophy of calmness, focus, and reverence.

---

## 1. The Reader (Quran, Hadith, Tafsir)
**Feeling:** *Immersive, Serene, Academic*
- **Visuals:** The interface entirely melts away. The screen is dominated by pristine typography and vast, comfortable margins. 
- **Personality:** It feels like holding a high-quality physical manuscript or a premium printed Mushaf.
- **Constraints:** Navigation and settings retract or fade when not in use. Toolbars are subtle and monochromatic. Distractions are explicitly engineered out of the viewport.

## 2. The Calculator (Zakat, Mirath, Kaffarah)
**Feeling:** *Trustworthy, Precise, Algorithmic*
- **Visuals:** Structured, aligned, and data-dense. Uses clear input fields, immediate visual feedback, and crisp structural dividers.
- **Personality:** It feels like a high-end financial dashboard or legal brief.
- **Constraints:** The layout uses wider grids to display tabular data. Output numbers are large, legible, and emphasized. Explanations (Fiqh rulings) are presented cleanly alongside inputs to build trust.

## 3. Knowledge Explorer (Graph, Timelines)
**Feeling:** *Expansive, Interconnected, Discoverable*
- **Visuals:** Fluid, spatial, and highly interactive. Uses connecting lines, nodes, and spatial mapping over deep, subdued backgrounds.
- **Personality:** It feels like a modern museum exhibit or an advanced academic research tool.
- **Constraints:** While visually complex, it must never feel chaotic. Data is clustered logically, and physics/animations are damped to feel smooth and intentional.

## 4. Settings & Preferences
**Feeling:** *Controlled, Predictable, Private*
- **Visuals:** Organized lists, clear toggles, and instant previews.
- **Personality:** It feels like the control deck of a premium operating system.
- **Constraints:** No complex nested menus if possible. Changes (like typography size or Dark Mode) apply instantly to provide immediate user confidence.

## 5. Search
**Feeling:** *Instant, Intelligent, Forgiving*
- **Visuals:** A massive, inviting input field centered on the screen, followed by cleanly grouped, categorized results (Quran, Hadith, Topics).
- **Personality:** It feels omniscient and highly capable.
- **Constraints:** Search results clearly highlight exactly *why* a node matched the query (e.g., highlighting the specific Arabic root or English keyword in context).

## 6. Landing & Dashboards (Daily Worship)
**Feeling:** *Welcoming, Habit-Forming, Contextual*
- **Visuals:** Soft modular cards, ambient greetings based on the time of day, and clear progress indicators.
- **Personality:** It feels like a helpful, deeply knowledgeable personal assistant guiding the user's daily spiritual life.
- **Constraints:** Information must be bite-sized. Daily tracking (Salah, Fasting) requires minimal taps—interaction should be frictionless.
