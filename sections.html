📜 Conditions for Qurbani

  • ✓ Muslim (male or female)
  • ✓ Sane (Aaqil) and adult (Baligh)
  • ✓ Resident (Muqeem — not a traveler)

🌙 Moon & Islamic Calendar

Allah says in the Quran: يَسْأَلُونَكَ عَنِ الْأَهِلَّةِ ۖ قُلْ هِيَ مَوَاقِيتُ لِلنَّاسِ وَالْحَجِّ

"They ask you about the crescents. Say: They are measurements of time for the people and for Hajj." — Al-Baqarah 2:189

  • 🌑 New Moon: Start of new Hijri month (after Hilal sighting)
  • 🌕 Full Moon: Mid-month (15 Ramadan, 15 Sha'ban)
  • 🌙 Lunar cycle: 29.5 days → 12 months = 354-day year

🏦 Islamic Finance Principles

🚫
Riba (Interest) Prohibited

Charging or paying interest is strictly forbidden. وَأَحَلَّ اللَّهُ الْبَيْعَ وَحَرَّمَ الرِّبَا — Al-Baqarah 2:275

🤝
Halal Trade Encouraged

Legitimate trade, profit-sharing (Mudarabah), partnerships (Musharakah) are encouraged in Islam.

⚖️
Gharar (Uncertainty) Avoided

Excessive uncertainty or speculation (options, futures) is prohibited. Investments must be in tangible assets.

Pillars of Islam & Iman

The foundational principles every Muslim lives by — the Five Pillars of Islam and Six Pillars of Iman.

☝️
الشهادة
1. Shahada (Declaration of Faith)

Bearing witness that there is no god but Allah and that Muhammad ﷺ is His messenger. The foundation of Islam.

لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ مُحَمَّدٌ رَسُولُ اللَّهِ
🙏
الصلاة
2. Salah (Prayer)

Five daily prayers — Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha. The direct connection between the worshiper and Allah.

إِنَّ الصَّلَاةَ كَانَتْ عَلَى الْمُؤْمِنِينَ كِتَابًا مَوْقُوتًا — 4:103
💰
الزكاة
3. Zakat (Charity)

Compulsory charity of 2.5% on accumulated wealth above Nisab. Purifies wealth and supports the community.

وَأَقِيمُوا الصَّلَاةَ وَآتُوا الزَّكَاةَ — 2:43
🌙
الصوم
4. Sawm (Fasting)

Fasting from dawn to sunset during Ramadan. Spiritual purification, gratitude, and empathy for the needy.

يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا كُتِبَ عَلَيْكُمُ الصِّيَامُ — 2:183
🕋
الحج
5. Hajj (Pilgrimage)

Pilgrimage to Mecca at least once in a lifetime for those who are able. The ultimate act of submission to Allah.

وَلِلَّهِ عَلَى النَّاسِ حِجُّ الْبَيْتِ مَنِ اسْتَطَاعَ إِلَيْهِ سَبِيلًا — 3:97
☝️
الإيمان بالله
1. Belief in Allah

Belief in the One God — His existence, oneness (Tawheed), names, attributes, and lordship.

قُلْ هُوَ اللَّهُ أَحَدٌ — Al-Ikhlas 112:1
👼
الإيمان بالملائكة
2. Belief in Angels

Belief in the angels — created from light, obedient to Allah, carrying out divine duties (Jibril, Mikail, Israfil, etc.).

وَالْمَلَائِكَةُ يُسَبِّحُونَ بِحَمْدِ رَبِّهِمْ — 42:5
📖
الإيمان بالكتب
3. Belief in Books

Belief in all divine scriptures — Torah (Tawrat), Psalms (Zabur), Gospel (Injeel), and the Quran (final revelation).

إِنَّا نَحْنُ نَزَّلْنَا الذِّكْرَ وَإِنَّا لَهُ لَحَافِظُونَ — 15:9
🕊️
الإيمان بالرسل
4. Belief in Prophets

Belief in all prophets from Adam AS to Muhammad ﷺ (the seal of prophets). 25 are named in the Quran.

مَا كَانَ مُحَمَّدٌ أَبَا أَحَدٍ مِن رِّجَالِكُمْ وَلَكِن رَّسُولَ اللَّهِ — 33:40
⏰
الإيمان باليوم الآخر
5. Belief in the Last Day

Belief in the Day of Judgment — resurrection (Ba'ath), accounting (Hisab), paradise (Jannah) and hellfire (Jahannam).

كُلُّ نَفْسٍ ذَائِقَةُ الْمَوْتِ — 3:185
📐
الإيمان بالقدر
6. Belief in Divine Decree

Belief in Qadar — that Allah has decreed all things in His infinite knowledge, both good and bad come from Him.

إِنَّا كُلَّ شَيْءٍ خَلَقْنَاهُ بِقَدَرٍ — 54:49
