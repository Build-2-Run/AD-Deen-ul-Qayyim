📜 Conditions for Qurbani

  • ✓ Muslim (male or female)
  • ✓ Sane (Aaqil) and adult (Baligh)
  • ✓ Resident (Muqeem — not a traveler)
  • ✓ Owner of Nisab (same as Zakat threshold)
  • ✓ On the days of Eid ul-Adha (10-13 Dhul Hijjah)
  • ✗ Cutting nails/hair before sacrificing (Sunnah to avoid)
💡 Note: Qurbani is obligatory (Wajib) or highly recommended (Sunnah Mu'akkadah) for eligible Muslims in their home city.
