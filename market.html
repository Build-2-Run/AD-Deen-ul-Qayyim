🏦 Islamic Finance Principles

🚫
Riba (Interest) Prohibited

Charging or paying interest is strictly forbidden. وَأَحَلَّ اللَّهُ الْبَيْعَ وَحَرَّمَ الرِّبَا — Al-Baqarah 2:275

🤝
Halal Trade Encouraged

Legitimate trade, profit-sharing (Mudarabah), partnerships (Musharakah) are encouraged in Islam.

⚖️
Gharar (Uncertainty) Avoided

Excessive uncertainty or speculation (options, futures) is prohibited. Investments must be in tangible assets.

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ
